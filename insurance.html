INVESTING IN YOUR MENTAL WELLNESS

Your mental health is one of the most important investments you will ever make. At Harmony NeuroCare, we believe cost should never be the reason someone cannot access safe, effective, and life-changing care. That is why we work closely with insurance partners, offer private pay options, and provide financial support programs that make treatments like Deep TMS, therapy, medication management, and ketamine more accessible.

Insurance Partners & Mental Health Coverage

Harmony NeuroCare accepts a wide range of insurance providers for treatments such as Deep TMS, SPRAVATO®, psychiatric evaluations, and other mental health services. Coverage varies by plan, but most insurers support Deep TMS for conditions including depression, anxious depression, late-life depression, and OCD, and many plans also offer benefits for SPRAVATO® when clinical criteria are met.

Our team verifies benefits, handles all paperwork, and guides you through the approval process so you know exactly what to expect before treatment begins.

PRIVATE PAY & FINANCING OPTIONS

Flexible solutions for your care

Harmony NeuroCare offers private pay options for all services, including Deep TMS, therapy, ketamine treatments, and psychiatric care. Clients can pay using cash, checks, major credit cards, HSA funds, or FSA accounts.

For those who prefer a payment plan, we partner with trusted financing providers that offer promotional zero percent financing for up to twenty-four months, subject to credit approval. These options make it easier to begin treatment while staying within your budget, and our team will help guide you toward the right resources for your situation.

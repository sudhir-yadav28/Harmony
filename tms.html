What actually happens to your brain during Deep TMS Therapy?

BrainsWay Deep TMS is an advanced, non-invasive brain stimulation treatment that gently activates underactive areas of the brain involved in mood, motivation, and emotional regulation. Unlike medication, which works systemically, Deep TMS targets the neurological source of symptoms with precision and without drugs or sedation.

This technology uses magnetic pulses (similar to those used in MRI machines) to stimulate deeper and broader regions of the brain than traditional figure-eight TMS devices. For many people living with depression, anxious-depression, or OCD, Deep TMS offers a science-backed path to relief when other treatments have not helped enough.

It is FDA cleared, evidence-based, and safe for adults and adolescents ages fifteen and older.

PROVEN RESULTS WHEN OTHER TREATMENTS FALL SHORT

Real relief backed by clinical research and patient outcomes

For many people, TMS is the turning point after years of trying medications, therapy, or lifestyle changes without lasting improvement. Research shows that TMS is four times more likely to achieve remission compared to placebo treatments, and it consistently outperforms traditional antidepressants in both effectiveness and long-term stability.

Most patients begin noticing improvements within a few weeks, with benefits that often last many months or even a year or more. Providers nationwide report response rates between 70 and 80 percent, and nearly half of patients achieve full remission with no remaining symptoms. These outcomes make Deep TMS one of the most successful treatments available in modern mental health care.

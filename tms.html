What actually happens to your brain during Deep TMS Therapy?

Deep TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain that control mood. In depression, these specific regions are often underactive. Deep TMS wakes them up, restoring their ability to communicate properly and regulate mood effectively.

Think of it like jump-starting a car battery or tuning an instrument—it helps the brain get back to its natural rhythm. Unlike medications, which travel through your entire body, TMS targets the source of the problem directly, with practically no systemic side effects.

FDA-Cleared • Non-Invasive • Proven Results

Medication backed by clinical research and patient outcomes

Clinical studies show that Deep TMS is highly effective, even for patients who have not found relief from antidepressants. Many patients experience significant reduction in symptoms, and a large percentage achieve full remission.

The Role of Medication in Mental Health Care

Medication can be a powerful tool for stabilizing symptoms, clearing the fog of depression, and creating a foundation for growth. But it is rarely the whole answer. We believe in "minimum effective dose" strategies that prioritize your long-term health over quick fixes.

HOW A COMBINED TREATMENT PROTOCOL OFFERS BEST RESULTS

Many patients find that combining treatments creates a synergistic effect. While medication works on neurochemistry, TMS works on electrical pathways. Addressing both can lead to faster, more robust remission rates than either treatment alone.

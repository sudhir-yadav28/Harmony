INNOVATIVE THERAPIES DESIGNED TO HELP YOU HEAL AND THRIVE

TMS Therapy

Featuring BrainsWay Deep TMS technology that reaches deeper brain regions and offers higher success rates for depression, anxiety, and OCD. Safe, non-invasive, and covered by most insurance plans.

LEARN MORE

Ketamine Therapy

Rapid-relief treatment used for depression and related conditions when traditional approaches have not provided enough improvement. Available through in-clinic ketamine care with personalized dosing and careful monitoring.

SPRAVATO®

An FDA-approved option for treatment-resistant depression that can provide faster symptom relief when standard antidepressants have not worked well enough. Administered in-clinic with supervision.

Med Management

Compassionate evaluations and medication management designed to stabilize symptoms and support long-term wellness with a minimum effective dose approach.

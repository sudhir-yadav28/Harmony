What is Ketamine Therapy?

Ketamine is a rapid-acting treatment that targets different pathways in the brain than traditional antidepressants. It works by blocking NMDA receptors and stimulating glutamate production, which helps the brain create new neural connections (neuroplasticity).

This process can effectively "reset" the brain, offering relief from symptoms of treatment-resistant depression, anxiety, and PTSD, often within hours or days rather than weeks.

How Ketamine Therapy
Complements Deep TMS

While Deep TMS works by stimulating specific brain regions over time to create structural strength, ketamine works rapidly to boost chemical messengers and create immediate flexibility. Using them together—or transitioning from one to the other—can provide a comprehensive "reset and rebuild" strategy for the brain.

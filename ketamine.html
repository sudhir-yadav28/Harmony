What Is Ketamine Therapy?

Ketamine therapy is a cutting-edge mental health treatment that uses low-dose ketamine to provide fast-acting relief for depression, anxiety, and other mood-based conditions, often offering hope when traditional medications and therapy haven’t worked. This treatment taps into different brain pathways than standard antidepressants, giving patients a chance for rapid relief and renewed perspective.

At Harmony NeuroCare, ketamine therapy is offered alongside other advanced modalities like Deep TMS, forming part of our Integrative Psychiatry Colorado approach to comprehensive mental health care.

SPRAVATO® TREATMENT AT HARMONY NEUROCARE

A supervised, FDA-approved option for treatment-resistant depression

SPRAVATO® is an FDA-approved esketamine nasal spray designed for adults with treatment-resistant depression who haven’t found relief with traditional antidepressants. It works through glutamate pathways rather than serotonin, creating faster shifts in mood and thought patterns. Each dose is administered in-clinic under provider supervision so you can feel supported and monitored throughout the process.

During your visit, you self-administer SPRAVATO® with guidance, then rest comfortably in a private room while our team observes your response. Treatment follows a structured schedule over several weeks, and many patients begin noticing changes early on. SPRAVATO® can be integrated with therapy, medication management, or Deep TMS as part of a personalized plan designed to support long-term healing.

Your Ketamine Treatment Journey

STEP 1: INITIAL CONSULT

Your journey begins with a comprehensive evaluation that explores your symptoms, history, and treatment goals. Together, we determine whether IV ketamine infusion, SPRAVATO®, or assisted psychotherapy is the most effective path forward.

Many clients leave this conversation feeling relieved to finally have a treatment option that offers rapid relief.

STEP 2: PREPARATION AND SAFETY REVIEW

Before your first session, your provider walks you through the entire process, discusses safety measures, and answers any questions. You receive instructions about how to prepare and what to expect during treatment.

This preparation helps most people feel grounded and confident going into their first session.

STEP 3: TREATMENT SESSION

Ketamine is administered slowly and safely, either by IV infusion, SPRAVATO® nasal spray, or within a therapeutic session. You remain awake and supported while your care team monitors you throughout.

Many people describe this stage as calming or introspective, with sensations fading shortly after the session ends.

STEP 4: INTEGRATION THERAPY

Optional integration sessions help you process the insights and experiences from your ketamine therapy, grounding the rapid changes into lasting shifts in perspective and mood.

Patients often find these sessions maximize their clinical outcomes.

STEP 5: ONGOING ASSESSMENTS

We regularly check in with scaling metrics to scientifically track your depression or anxiety scores, making sure the treatment protocol aligns with your goals and progress.

This keeps your care grounded in tangible evidence.

STEP 6: MAINTENANCE PLAN

Once you have completed the acute phase and feel better, we will build a long-term maintenance plan, including lifestyle coaching or occasional "booster" treatments to keep you feeling well.

Healing is an ongoing journey, and we're here for it.

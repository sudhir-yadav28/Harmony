Dual Diagnosis Care

Trauma and addiction are frequently intertwined. Substance use often begins as a way to self-medicate profound pain and anxiety.

At Harmony Neurocare, we treat the root causes—the trauma itself—recognizing that true healing requires addressing both simultaneously.

Recalibrating Brain Networks

REDUCING CRAVINGS WITH TMS

Chronic addiction and trauma alter reward circuitry. Deep TMS acts as a vital tool to recalibrate these specific networks, helping reduce the intensity of cravings and mood symptoms.

Reclaim Your Life

You don't have to carry the weight of trauma and addiction alone. Our team provides the expert care needed to support your recovery.
